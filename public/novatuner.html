Nova Tuner

Coming soon. Please send a message if you'd like to try the beta version.

Written for professionals, especially those in the field of Early Music, Nova Tuner is a fully featured, highly accurate, chromatic tuner with extensive temperaments support.

Main Features

  • Clean and simple interface.
  • Sound Mode (reference pitch) and Auto Mode (detect pitch).
  • About 45 Built-in temperaments. (See below)
  • Add new temperaments.
  • Duplicate and edit built-in temperaments.
  • Quick selection of common pitch levels: 392, 415, 430, and 440 Hz (these are customizable).
  • No data is collected, no internet connection is required.
  • Custom temperaments are saved locally, as well as synced to your other iOS devices via iCloud when internet is available.

Interface

  • All tuner settings (pitch level, temperament, etc) clearly visible on the main screen.
  • Standard iOS appearance: the intuitive controls you expect.
  • Large and intuitive adjustment buttons (no fussy scroll wheels).
  • Light and dark mode.
  • Supports portrait and landscape orientations.
  • Note names in English, German, and Neo-Latin (do, re, mi).

Temperaments

  • Add, duplicate and edit temperaments.
  • Favorite frequently used temperaments.
  • Filter by favorited and custom.
  • Search entire list of temperaments by name or information (creator, type, year, etc.).
  • Hide unwanted temperaments so they don't appear in the list or search.

Built-in Temperaments

  • 1/8 Pythagorean Well Temperament
  • Bach: Barnes, Hill, Kellner, Lehman, Louie, O'Donnell, Swich
  • Bendeler I, II, and III
  • Boulliau Pythagorean
  • De Morgan
  • Egarr's English Ord
  • Equal Temperament
  • French: Corrette, D'Alembert, Rameau, Rousseau
  • Grammateus Pythagorean
  • Handel
  • Kirnberger II and III
  • Lambert
  • Marpourg
  • Meantone: 1/3, 1/4, 1/5, 1/6, and 2/7 comma
  • Neidhardt: "Kleine Stadt" and "Grosse Stadt"
  • Pinnock Modern
  • Prelleur
  • Pythagorean
  • Vallotti (Original and Pythagorean Comma versions)
  • Werckmeister III, IV, V, and Septenarius
  • Young I and II

Specifications

Transposition range -11 to +11 semitones
Detection range A0 to C8
Reference tone C2 to B7
Waveforms Sawtooth, Square, Sine, Triangle
Tuning modes Sound, Auto, Auto with Sound Back
Calibration range A4=362-470 Hz (1.0 or 0.1 Hz steps)

Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, transfer, or use any user data.
