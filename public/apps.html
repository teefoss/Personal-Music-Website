Music Apps

iOS apps by Thomas Foster

Nova Tuner

Coming soon. Please send a message if you'd like to try the beta version.

Nova Tuner is a chromatic tuner with extension support for temperaments, designed especially with historically informed performers in mind.

  • Many built-in temperaments, with the ability to filter by favorite or search.
  • Add your own temperaments.
  • Duplicate built-in temperaments and edit them.

Allegro Metronome

Available on the App Store.

Allegro Metronome is a response to the overly complicated metronomes that populate the App Store. It's the equivalent of a classic metronome, but a with clean, modern, and easy-to-use interface.

  • Large plus and minus buttons for easy adjustment when practicing.
  • Only the BPM values found on a real metronome (...60, 63, 66, etc.). (Real metronomes do not have BPM values that increment by 1 — there is very little audible difference between 60 BPM and 61 BPM, for example.)
  • The time signature feature that is found on most electronic metronomes has been replace with an audible beat subdivision, which is much for useful, for example for very slow practice.
